Darkin Gallery: The Faces of Destruction


The Darkins are once divine beings from Shurima. They were Ascended gods, but the madness from their war against the Void ultimately consumed them. Over time, their souls were corrupted, transforming them into their own unique, twisted, and monstrous forms. After nearly bringing about the destruction of the world through their brutal wars and new powers, the greatest mages of Runeterra succeeded in imprisoning their souls within the weapons they once wielded. Now trapped within these weapons, the Darkins call out to lure unsuspecting victims to possess them, driven by a new, dark purpose.
